## Content (standard, abstract-attested)
Proves the irrationality of the Erdős-Borwein constant `E = Σ_{n=1}^∞ 1/(2^n − 1) ≈ 1.606695…`. Method: divisor-function techniques specific to the structure of the 2^n − 1 series.
