# lit-015 — Wieting (2008), "A Khinchin Sequence"

**Status:** unverified_abstract_only · **Class:** secondary_paper

## Citation
Wieting, T. (2008). _A Khinchin Sequence._ Proceedings of the American Mathematical Society **136**, pp. 815–824. DOI `10.1090/S0002-9939-07-09202-7`.

## Content (abstract-attested)
Constructs an explicit Khinchin sequence — a specific continued fraction whose partial quotients realize K_0 as their limiting geometric mean. This is a positive constructive complement to BBC 1997: BBC §3 had noted no such explicit construction was known at time of writing; Wieting 2008 provides one.

## Access
AMS Proc journal; abstract free at AMS, full text institutional. Not retrieved this session.

## Mission role
Shows that K_0 is concretely realizable as a geometric-mean limit (positive complement to the BBC null on arithmetic nature). **Not load-bearing** for M2.3 — Wieting's construction does NOT address K_0's algebraic / transcendental status.
