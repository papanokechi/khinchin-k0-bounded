## Citation
Khinchin, A. Ya. (1963/1964). _Continued Fractions._ English translation, University of Chicago Press 1964; reprint Dover Publications 1997, ISBN 0-486-69630-8. (Russian original P. Noordhoff Ltd., Groningen 1963 from 1935-onward Russian editions.)

## Content (standard)
Monograph treatment of regular continued fractions: convergents, Diophantine approximation, metric theory, Khinchin's theorem (Ch. 3 of most editions), ergodic-theory-precursor proofs.
