# lit-007 — Khinchin (1935), "Metrische Kettenbruchprobleme"

**Status:** unverified_abstract_only · **Class:** primary_paper
**Foundational citation for the entire mission.**

## Citation
Khinchin, A. Ya. (1935). _Metrische Kettenbruchprobleme._ Compositio Mathematica **1**, pp. 361–382. (German.)

## Content (transitively known via secondary sources)
- Original statement and proof of Khinchin's theorem: for Lebesgue-a.e. real x, geometric mean of partial quotients in regular CF of x converges to K_0.
- Closed-form expression for K_0 in terms of a Hurwitz-zeta-type series.
- Pre-ergodic-theory proof — uses metric/measure-theoretic arguments directly. (Modern proof via GKW operator is Ryll-Nardzewski 1951 and later — see lit-011.)
- English translation appears in Khinchin's 1963/64 book _Continued Fractions_ — see lit-008.
