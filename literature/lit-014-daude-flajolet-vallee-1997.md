# lit-014 — Daudé, Flajolet, & Vallée (1997)

**Status:** theoretical_citation_only · **Class:** theoretical_obstruction_citation_only

## Citation
Daudé, H., Flajolet, P., and Vallée, B. (1997). _An average-case analysis of the Gaussian algorithm for continued fractions._ Combinatorics, Probability and Computing **6**, pp. 397–433.

## Content (abstract-attested)
Analytic combinatorics of GKW-type transfer operators applied to the average-case analysis of continued-fraction-based algorithms. Provides the analytical machinery that would be needed for a GKW-route attack on K_0.
