# lit-013 — Vallée (2003/2006), Euclidean / continued-fraction dynamics survey

**Status:** theoretical_citation_only · **Class:** survey

## Citation
Vallée, B. (2006). _Euclidean dynamics._ Discrete and Continuous Dynamical Systems **15**, pp. 281–352. (Companion survey: Vallée, B. (2003), _Dynamical analysis of a class of Euclidean algorithms_, Theoretical Computer Science **297**, pp. 447–486.)

## Content (abstract-attested)
Comprehensive survey of spectral theory of transfer operators for continued-fraction-class dynamical systems. Includes the GKW operator and its generalizations. Provides treatment of the obstructions to direct spectral-theoretic attacks on K_0's arithmetic nature.
