# lit-012 — Wirsing (1974)

## Citation
Wirsing, E. (1974). _On the theorem of Gauss-Kuzmin-Lévy and a Frobenius-type theorem for function spaces._ Acta Arithmetica **24**, pp. 507–528.

## Content (standard)
Frobenius-Perron-type theorem for the GKW transfer operator on Banach spaces of analytic functions, determining the second eigenvalue ≈ `0.30366300289873265860…` (Wirsing's constant), which controls the rate of convergence in the Gauss-Kuzmin distribution.
