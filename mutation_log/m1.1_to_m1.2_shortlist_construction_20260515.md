a sweep substitution, a content-deficient milestone now forces a §7 review
rather than a quiet pass. Both protect against Searcher's Fatigue along
different axes (compute-substitution vs process-substitution).

- `T` Tractability of bounded sub-question

desc → problem-name alphabetical.
